Tonga

170 islands make up the whole Kingdom of Tonga. From that 170 the main islands my family is from are, Lofanga, Niua fo'ou, and Kolovai. The capital of Tonga is Nuku'alofa.

Tonga is a very beautiful place but, very strict. Tonga is very strict on respect, and especially strict on Sabbath. On Sabbath all streets are empty, stores and shops are closed, and the only thing people do is go church, come home, eat then rest to show.

Israel

Israel is the birthplace of the Jewish culture. I don't really know much about my Israelian side. I want to learn more about this part of my culture and hopefully one day reconnect with my roots in Israel.

In Israel they call sabbath "Shabbat". Shabbat is just like Seventh-day sabbath. Start from Friday sunset to Saturday sunset. Shabbat is a day where Jewish people are reminded of the story of creation where God created the world in 6 days and rested on the 7th.
